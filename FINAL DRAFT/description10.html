All of Us Are Dead

All of Us Are Dead is a gripping South Korean Netflix series about a zombie apocalypse that erupts at a high school, trapping students who must fight for survival, confront complex friendships, and navigate intense moral choices while waiting for rescue from a world suddenly plunged into chaos. Based on a popular webtoon, the show blends teen drama, horror, and thrilling action as friends like Nam On-jo and Lee Cheong-san battle fast-moving zombies and internal conflicts, exploring themes of bullying, love, and determination.
