The Scape of the Seven

The Escape of the Seven is a South Korean thriller drama about seven people from different backgrounds who become entangled in the mysterious disappearance and subsequent death of a young girl, Bang Da-mi, driven by greed and deception, leading to a bloody revenge plot orchestrated by a mastermind, Matthew Lee, as they face divine retribution for their sins. The series, known for its over-the-top twists and melodrama, follows these individuals as they betray and deceive each other in a game designed to expose their wicked deeds, with a sequel, The Escape of the Seven: Resurrection, continuing the story of vengeance and survival.
