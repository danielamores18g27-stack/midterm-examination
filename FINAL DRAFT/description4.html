Revenge of Others

Revenge of Others is a Korean teen thriller about shooting champion Ok Chan-mi, who transfers to her twin brother's high school after his suspicious death, determined to find his killer, teaming up with fellow student Ji Soo-heon, who secretly enacts justice for bullied students, to uncover the truth and get revenge on those responsible, blurring lines between vigilante justice and vengeance.
