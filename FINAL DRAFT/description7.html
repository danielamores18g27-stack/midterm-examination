Penthouse

The Penthouse: War in Life is a K-drama about the intense rivalries, secrets, and revenge among wealthy families living in the luxurious Hera Palace, centered around three women: the graceful Shim Su-ryeon (seeking justice), the ambitious soprano Cheon Seo-jin (ruthless pursuit of status), and the determined Oh Yoon-hee (striving for her daughter's success). Their lives intertwine through scandals, crimes (including murder), and a cutthroat battle for power and social standing, especially concerning their children's education and futures.
