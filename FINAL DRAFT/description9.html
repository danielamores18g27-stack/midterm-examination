Little Women

The plot of the K-drama Little Women (2022) follows three impoverished sisters—In-joo, In-kyung, and In-hye—who become entangled in a massive conspiracy involving Korea's wealthiest family after the eldest sister finds a bag of money left by a friend who seemingly committed suicide, leading them into a shadowy world of secrets, power, and a sinister orchid society that controls powerful figures. The series blends family drama with a thrilling mystery as the sisters navigate financial struggles, corruption, and a dangerous game with the elite, fighting for independence while uncovering dark truths about their society and the powerful Park family.
